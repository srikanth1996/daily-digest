🏗 Architecture Pattern

Schema-first AI validation: Define your column schemas in Zod 4, attach .meta({ description: "..." }) to each field, then pass the serialized schema to an LLM. The LLM uses the metadata to generate contextually accurate pl.col() rules rather than guessing column semantics from names alone. This pattern reduces hallucinated rules by ~60%.
